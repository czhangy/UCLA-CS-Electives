Stephen Curry

Bio

Stephen Curry spent his 3-year college career with the Davidson Wildcats before being drafted in 2009 to the Golden State Warriors. He has since forged a legacy as the greast shooter and Warrior of all-time and one of the best players in NBA history.

Accolades

  1. 4x NBA Champion
  2. 1x Finals MVP
  3. 1x WCF MVP
  4. 2x MVP
  5. 1x All-Star Game MVP
  6. 9x All-NBA
  7. 9x All-Star
  8. 2x MVP
  9. 2x Scoring Leader
  10. 1x Steals Leader
  11. 1x 50/40/90
